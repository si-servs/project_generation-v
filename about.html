Felicia Simento

Founder & CEO

Who is Felicia Simento

Felicia is a business leader with 8 years of experience in the healthcare industry. Her passion for helping people is innate and Felicia believes all generations should be treated with respect and dignity. Felicia holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Nursing from York University and is a qualified a nurse. Her past experiences at Bridgepoint active healthcare and Sunnybrook hospital gave her insight into the loneliness, and unhappiness that can stem from a feeling of being left behind. Generation V is a change agent, that will have a profound impact on the lives of your loved ones.

Fennella Simento

Operations Manager

Who is Fennella Simento

Fennella has been passionate about improving senior care for years. With a career focused in business operations at major Canadian organizations. She has been focused on providing operational excellence in the industry and delivering unmatched senior experiences that seamlessly integrate exceptional hospitality, cutting-edge technology, innovative wellness programs, and world-class care. Fennella earned a Bachelor of Business Management from Ryerson University. Later she undertook a Diploma in Marketing from the University of Toronto.
